From Recess to Efficiency:How Online Games are Boosting Effectiveness
12-08-2024 | Games, Productivity
Humanity has come a long way with regard to video games. Starting with the first one, Pong which now seems rudimentary; up to recent AAA blockbusters such as Call of Duty and Fortnite, video games have become an inseparable part of our culture. While some tend to consider them as playing futilely and running away from more important commitments, there are indications that even video games could help individuals achieve better results in their work. In this article, we will be discussing how they can boost productivity using various examples for illustration purposes.
Enhancement of cognitive abilities
One primary advantage of playing video games is that it enhances your cognitive skills. Several studies have shown that playing video games may improve problem-solving ability, decision-making and critical thinking amongst others. For instance, a recent study by the University of Rochester utilized action video games to demonstrate how being exposed to fast-paced visual stimuli can influence perception and thus judgment making processes. The research found out that people who played action video games could make 25% faster accurate decisions as compared to those who didn’t game at all.
In the journal Nature, another study has discovered that video games may help you multitask better. The study participants were asked to play a game where they had to switch between tasks. The findings showed that gamers could switch from one activity to another faster and more accurately than non-gamers. This ability of better multitasking may make them more productive in real life situations like schools or workplace settings.

Lessening Tension
A significant part of decline in efficiency is caused by the stress we experience. Work becomes difficult to concentrate on and tasks may not be finished during periods of tension. However video games aid in alleviating strain and increasing output. When people engage in virtual entertainment, their brains generate dopamine, which is associated with feelings of mirth, compensation for good behavior etc. By triggering dopamine release from the brain regions responsible for pleasure, it helps to ease anxiety and improves one’s state of mind.
Also, playing videogames can literally offer a temporal break from the stressors of modern living. Video games offer us a virtual place where we are able to fully engage with it as per own will. It may be an enjoyable distraction from work or class, helping us regain some energy before heading back to tackle them again.
Example
  • Stardew Valley: this is an inspiring agricultural-like simulation game that calms organs and has a curative potential. Players are able to handle land, hedge garden, as well as socialize with the residents of a selected region. It’s a game due to which one can live in peace instead of worrying.
  • Animal Crossing: In this realistic simulation game about life, you move into a new town in the company of animal citizens. According to several reviews, it is one of the best means that we can use to relieve stress after long tiring days due to its peaceful atmosphere.
    • Growth of motivation
    Productivity is greatly affected by motivation. When we are inspired, we tend to put more effort into our work and pay more attention to our goals. It could be said that video games can serve as effective motivators because they give us satisfaction and improvement. Videogames typically have specific aims and ambitions, so that once players finish their tasks or missions they feel a certain sense of accomplishment.
    In addition, video gaming will often provide us with fast feedback and incentives; thus they can motivate us effectively. Normally, as we accomplish a task or aim in the game we earn points, rewards among other types of motivations. This feeling of reward may increase our motivation and determination to complete tasks in real-life situations.
    Example
  • Habitica: Habitica uses your responsibilities as a game in order for you to become more productive. You can create your own avatar, and then get rewards when you complete tasks and develop good habits. It is an application that has won many awards for its capacity of turning boring things into games so as to make them more interesting and motivational.
    • Expanding imaginative power
    Another significant part of being able to work productively is creativity. When creative thought moments happen, we’re likely to think of other solutions and proposals. Playing video games can foster imagination, as they require young men and men to think of innovative solutions for them or consider unconventional ideas.
    An instance of such creativity is seen in Mine-craft and Terra-rium where individual players can create their own spaces and buildings with their own designs. In other types of games like puzzlers or strategy games, players are required to engage their brains more than ever before when it comes down to resolving issues and overcoming obstacles.

Video games has been reported to enhance mental capabilities, correct stress levels, spur up motivation, foster innovativeness as well as promote effective socializing skills leading to elevated productivity in physical world.
